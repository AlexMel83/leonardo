Training for applicants

If you dream of a professional career in the field of art, Art Studio "Leonardo" offers you effective training for admission to higher educational institutions in Ukraine and abroad.

We help applicants understand the requirements of higher education institutions and prepare for entrance exams. Applicants are trained by a professional teacher-methodologist.

Our courses are designed for different levels of training, from beginners to experienced artists. We offer preparation for entrance tests of various levels of difficulty.
