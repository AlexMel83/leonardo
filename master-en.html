Our master classes

Art workshops at the "Leonardo" art studio provide an opportunity to discover your talent and express your feelings through art.

They allow children to learn different artistic techniques, introduce them to different styles and genres.

Positive emotions that arise during art workshops help to relieve stress, calm and provide inner harmony.

"Spring Rabbit"

Thematic spring modeling workshop for children from 7 years old.
In this master class, children sculpt a relief from plasticine of wonderful spring bunnies.

Plasticine modeling has many advantages for children. It promotes the development of motor skills and hand coordination, improves their creative imagination and spatial thinking. In addition, this activity promotes the development of concentration and attention in children, as it requires accuracy and thoughtfulness.

The format of the work is A5, all materials are provided.

"A gift to the nearest and dearest"

Acrylic painting master class for children from 7 years old.
We draw a picture that will be a great gift for beloved mothers, grandmothers, sisters!

Working with acrylic paints helps children develop artistic skills and the ability to control a brush. They learn to mix colors, apply layers of paint and create different ones texture on the canvas. This contributes to the development of their motor skills, coordination of movements and the ability to detail.

All materials are provided.
