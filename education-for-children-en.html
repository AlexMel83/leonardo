Education for children

Education for children in the art studio "Leonardo" is an exciting journey into the world of creativity and art.

The teachers of the art studio "Leonardo" have developed a special program on fine arts for children. Each lesson involves a new interesting topic to consolidate basic knowledge on the following topics:

  • composition;
  • drawing;
  • painting;
  • sculpture;
  • molding.

Our students regularly participate in exhibitions and competitions, which gives them the opportunity to show their talents and gain recognition.
