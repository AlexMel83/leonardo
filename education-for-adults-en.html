Education for adults

Art studio "Leonardo" offers training for adults who dream of developing their creative abilities and learning new artistic techniques. Our teachers are professional masters of their field who will help you develop your creative potential.

Our courses give you the opportunity not only to improve your artistic skills, but also to find new friends and colleagues in the field of art. We create a comfortable and creative atmosphere where you can develop and improve your artistic abilities.

Drawing courses for adults will be useful not only for those who already have independent experience creativity, but also to those who want to start their studies from the very basics. The training program includes all successive stages of art education and is based on an individual approach to each student.
